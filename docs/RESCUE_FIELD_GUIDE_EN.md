# Respuesta Venezuela · Guide for rescue teams

> Quick field guide. Built to be read from a phone, in minutes, while you work.
> Spanish version: [RESCUE_FIELD_GUIDE_ES.md](RESCUE_FIELD_GUIDE_ES.md)

## What is it?

A tool that combines **satellite imagery** with **artificial intelligence** to point out where the **critical structural damage** is in Venezuela after the earthquake.

It saves time: it does not rely on social-media reports. It surfaces the areas where buildings most likely collapsed, so you can decide where to look first.

## How to use it

1. **Open** [https://respuestavenezuela.org/](https://respuestavenezuela.org/) on your phone.
   If you don't have stable internet, use the **Download / Install** button to save the page and use it **offline**.

2. **Look at the areas on the map.** You will see:
   - 🔴 **Red points** = estimated **critical structural damage** (total collapse, people likely trapped).
   - 🟡 **Yellow points** = estimated **partial damage** (damage present, but the structure is still standing).
   - 🖼️ **Before/after imagery** = you can visually verify whether the AI got it wrong.

3. **Verify before sending resources.** The AI helps, but it is **not perfect**. In some cases the map may mark a structure red that is in fact still standing. Use the before/after imagery and your judgment on the ground before mobilizing a team.

4. **Language.** At the top you can switch between **ES / EN** depending on who is looking at the screen.
